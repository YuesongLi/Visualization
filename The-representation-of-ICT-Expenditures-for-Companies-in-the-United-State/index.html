InfoVis Final Project(Fall 2015)

Information&Communication Technology Visualization

Zexin Zhao, Yiyuan Zhang, Yuesong Li, Maryam Karimi

Abstract

As information and communication Technology (ICT) broadly develops, more and more companies cost much on ICT construction. In our visualization system, user can overview the dataset structure, derive and compare the total expenditures for different uses of ICT expenditure in each year, and do the trend analysis. The dataset is collected by the Information and Communication Technology Survey (ICTS). And this survey covered All domestic, private, non-farm, including agricultural non-farm (NAICS Subsectors 113, 114 and 115) businesses.These data can greatly help not only ICT technology suppliers to decide the main direction of their business, but also the decision makers from Industry and government to get know about development trends of ICT, and then apply ICT to improve business and Economy.
