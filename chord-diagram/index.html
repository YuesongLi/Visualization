The Representation of Books about US politics

Arcs represent books about US politics sold by the online bookseller Amazon.com. chords represent frequent co-purchasing of books by the same buyers, as indicated by the "customers who bought this book also bought these other books" feature on Amazon.

Arcs have been given "Class" values "l", "n", or "c" to indicate whether they are "liberal", "neutral", or "conservative". These alignments were assigned separately by Mark Newman based on a reading of the descriptions and reviews of the books posted on Amazon
